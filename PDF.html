INTERNAL DIRECTIVE: ATTENDANCE & DISCIPLINARY PENALTIES

TO: ALL COUNCIL MEMBERS

•

FROM: THE SECRETARIAT

This official memorandum serves as a formal notification regarding the immediate enforcement of revised attendance regulations. Following a strategic review of our project momentum and organizational discipline, the Executive Council has ratified the following mandatory protocols for all scheduled meetings.

It has become imperative to ensure that every member of the council contributes effectively to the progress of the School of Computer Science Hub. As such, the following financial penalties shall be strictly enforced without prejudice:

01

Unexcused Absence & Late Notice

Any member who is absent from a meeting without a tangible, verifiable reason, or fails to notify the General Secretary at least forty-eight (48) hours prior to the meeting, shall be liable to a fine of ₦2,000.00.

02

Chronic Absenteeism

Continued negligence of duty as evidenced by three (3) consecutive absences shall attract a severe penalty of ₦5,000.00. Failure to remit this fine may result in a formal suspension of executive privileges.

Members are reminded that "tangible reasons" are limited to medical emergencies or unavoidable academic commitments. All excuses must be backed by evidence if requested by the Internal Audit Unit. Fines are to be paid into the Council’s treasury within 48 hours of the offense.

*** THIS REGULATION IS EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY ***
