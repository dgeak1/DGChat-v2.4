Official Directive

To: All Executive Council Members & Stakeholders
From: Office of the Secretariat
Subject: New Regulations Regarding Meeting Attendance and Penalties

This memorandum serves as an official notice to all members regarding the newly ratified rules governing attendance at scheduled meetings. To maintain organizational discipline and project momentum, the following regulations are effective immediately.

Attendance Penalties:

1

Unexcused Absence: Any member absent without a tangible reason or failing to inform the group at least two (2) days prior to the meeting shall incur a fine of ₦2,000.

2

Chronic Absenteeism: Any member who records three (3) consecutive absences from meetings shall incur a cumulative fine of ₦5,000.

Please note that "tangible reasons" are subject to verification by the Secretariat. All fines must be cleared within 48 hours of the concluded meeting to avoid further disciplinary action.

The rule takes effect immediately upon the issuance of this notice.
